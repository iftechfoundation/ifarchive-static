Privacy Policy (Summary)

This web site does not attempt to track you in any way. The IF Archive does not use cookies.

If you submit content to the Archive, you will be ask to provide your name and email address. We retain this information permanently in order to track the provenance of the material we host.

This site is hosted by Linode, with content distribution and caching by CloudFlare.

This web site retains log files, which include your IP address and the page requested. This information is retained for 30 days in order to monitor web traffic.

We will not share any of your personal information with any other individual or business, except as noted here.

If you wish your personal information to be deleted or forgotten, please contact us at info@ifarchive.org.
